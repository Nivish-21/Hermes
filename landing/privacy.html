PRIVACY

Private by default.

When you request beta access, Switchboard stores your Telegram numeric account identifier and your application state so we can review and manage access.

What beta access can do

Approved beta users can submit private research requests and view their own request status and cost information. They cannot access shared workspace actions or the public proof dashboard.

What we do not expose

We do not expose requester identity, request text, task parameters, credentials, or internal identifiers through the public proof dashboard.

Questions or deletion requests

Contact the Switchboard team through the same private Telegram chat used for beta access. We will review account access and deletion requests manually during the beta.
