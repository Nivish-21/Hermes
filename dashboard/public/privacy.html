PRIVACY POLICY

Private by default.

Effective July 12, 2026 · Last updated July 12, 2026

1. Who operates Switchboard

Switchboard is an invite-only beta operated by Nivish Vincent Raj in Tamil Nadu, India. Privacy and deletion requests may be sent to nivishv2004@gmail.com or through the private Telegram bot conversation.

2. Information we collect

  • Registration details: email address, and optional name and company.
  • Telegram beta details: numeric Telegram user ID and approval status.
  • Research inputs and outputs: questions, generated briefs, citations, status, cost, and execution telemetry.
  • Security data: rate-limit counters keyed by one-way hashes of email/network identifiers. We do not intentionally store the raw network address in the rate-limit table.
  • Operational logs supplied by Convex, Cloudflare, Telegram, OpenAI, and Linkup.

3. How we use information

We use information to review beta applications, authenticate permitted Telegram users, provide and verify research, prevent abuse, troubleshoot the beta, measure cost and reliability, enforce our Terms, and respond to privacy requests. Registration does not enroll you in promotional email.

4. Public dashboard

The public proof dashboard exposes only sanitized run status, model/cost telemetry, timing, and trace structure. It does not intentionally expose requester identity, request text, task parameters, evidence, credentials, Telegram IDs, email addresses, or internal request/task identifiers.

5. Service providers and international processing

We use service providers including Telegram, Convex, Cloudflare, OpenAI, and Linkup. Information may be processed in countries outside your state or country. Each provider processes data under its own terms and privacy practices. We do not sell personal information.

6. Retention

Registration and beta-access records are retained while the beta is active and for up to 12 months after the last relevant activity unless a longer period is required for security, dispute, or legal reasons. Private research requests and associated operational records are targeted for deletion or de-identification within 90 days after the beta need ends. Rate-limit records expire operationally when their one-hour window is no longer needed and may be removed during routine maintenance.

7. Your choices and rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or withdrawal from the beta by emailing nivishv2004@gmail.com. We will verify the request and target completion within 30 days, subject to security and legal retention needs. Depending on your US state or country, additional privacy rights may apply. We do not discriminate for exercising applicable rights.

8. Security

We use allowlists, invite approval, least-privilege public queries, hashed rate-limit keys, secret separation, and bounded external actions. No system is perfectly secure; do not submit secrets, regulated records, or highly sensitive personal information during the beta.

9. Children

The beta is intended for people aged 18 or older and is not directed to children. Contact us if you believe a child submitted information.

10. Cookies and tracking

The landing page does not intentionally use advertising cookies or cross-site behavioral tracking. Infrastructure providers may use essential technical data to deliver and secure the service.

11. Changes

We may update this policy as the beta changes. Material updates will be reflected by the “Last updated” date and, when practical, communicated through the beta channel.
